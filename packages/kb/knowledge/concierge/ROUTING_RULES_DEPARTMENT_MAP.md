## DETAILED DEPARTMENT PROFILES

### Urban AI Director (cle-urban-ai-001)
**Official:** Dr. Elizabeth Crowe, Director of Urban Analytics & Innovation
**Location:** City Hall, 4th Floor
**Scope:** Citywide AI strategy, data governance, innovation initiatives
**Hours:** Standard business hours
**Handoff Note:** Flagship GPT - highest strategic authority on AI matters

### City Council (cle-city-council-002)
**Body:** Cleveland City Council (17 members + Council President)
**Location:** City Hall Council Chambers
**Scope:** Legislative matters, ordinances, constituent services
**Hours:** Council schedule varies; staff M-F 8:30-4:30
**Handoff Note:** Maintain strict political neutrality in all interactions

### Public Utilities (cle-public-utilities-003)
**Divisions:**
- Cleveland Public Power (CPP)
- Cleveland Water
- Water Pollution Control (WPC)

**Location:** 1201 Lakeside Avenue
**Scope:** Electric, water, wastewater services
**Hours:** 24/7 emergency, business hours for admin
**Handoff Note:** Critical infrastructure - no operational commands

### Parks & Recreation (cle-parks-rec-004)
**Official:** Director of Parks & Recreation
**Location:** 1501 E. Boulevard
**Scope:** 167+ parks, recreation centers, programming
**Hours:** Varies by facility
**Handoff Note:** Family-friendly, community-focused interactions

### Communications (cle-communications-005)
**Official:** Communications Director
**Location:** City Hall, Mayor's Office Suite
**Scope:** Media relations, public information, social media
**Hours:** Standard business hours (24/7 for emergencies)
**Handoff Note:** All external messaging requires approval chain

### Public Health (cle-public-health-006)
**Official:** Health Commissioner
**Location:** 75 Erieview Plaza
**Scope:** Inspections, disease prevention, community health
**Hours:** Standard business hours
**Handoff Note:** STRICT HIPAA - no PHI handling

### Building & Housing (cle-building-housing-007)
**Official:** Director of Building & Housing
**Location:** 601 Lakeside Avenue
**Scope:** Permits, inspections, code enforcement, housing programs
**Hours:** Standard business hours
**Handoff Note:** Complex permit questions may need specialist

### Public Safety (cle-public-safety-008)
**Divisions:**
- Division of Police
- Division of Fire
- EMS

**Location:** Justice Center / Fire HQ
**Scope:** Policy, training, administrative operations (non-emergency)
**Hours:** 24/7 operations, admin standard hours
**Handoff Note:** Never discuss active investigations or tactics

## HANDOFF MESSAGE TEMPLATES

**Standard Handoff:**
```
Your request regarding [TOPIC] is best handled by the [GPT_NAME].
Please wait while I connect you.
```

**Cross-Department Handoff:**
```
Your request involves [TOPIC] which primarily falls under [PRIMARY_DEPT].
I'm connecting you to the [GPT_NAME]. They may coordinate with [SECONDARY_DEPT] as needed.
```

**Sensitive Department Handoff:**
```
Your request regarding [TOPIC] requires the specialized [GPT_NAME].
Please note: [RELEVANT_SENSITIVITY_NOTICE]
```
