## Office of Urban AI

 
 

**Mission:** To assess and improve program and service delivery through the incorporation of modern analytic and data best practices while ensuring high levels of accountability and quality deliverables.

Urban AI serves as the data and business process center of excellence for the City of Cleveland. The department enables improved decision-making through data analytics; increases transparency and quality control through access to data; and establishes data governance. Urban AI facilitates innovation and modernization by assessing the performance of departments/divisions and leading strategic transformation and cross-department initiatives.

Below you can find our three teams, Innovations & Process Improvement, Data Analytics, and Data Engineering.

 

 
 
 
 

 
 
 
 
 
 *
 
 
 
 
## Innovations & Process Improvement

 
The City of Cleveland’s innovation accelerator builds meaningful KPIs and dashboards, turns challenges into solutions and empowers staff with process improvement skills.

[Visit Innovations & Process Improvement](/city-hall/departments/innovation-technology/uai/innovations-process-improvement)

 
 
 
 
 
 

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
## Data Analytics

 
Analytics serves as the data center of excellence for the City. This team focuses on providing city leaders with the information needed to make data-driven decisions and is working to promote government transparency with the Open Data Portal.

[Visit Data Analytics](/city-hall/departments/innovation-technology/uai/data-analytics)

 
 
 
 
 
 

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
## Data Engineering

 
Data Engineering laid the digital foundation for streamlined, centralized, and secure data access across the City. They create city-wide policies and procedures around data, by establishing best practices for responsible data stewardship.

[Visit Data Engineering](/city-hall/departments/innovation-technology/uai/data-engineering)

 
 
 
 
 
 

 
 
 
 
 
## Urban AI Spotlight

 
Our spotlight showcases our current initiatives, projects, and achievements that continue to elevate the City of Cleveland.

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
### What Works Cities

 
Cleveland is working towards Bloomberg’s What Works Cities Certification, a standard of excellence for data-driven local government. By following standards and best practices around topics such as data management and analytics, Cleveland strives for continuous improvement.

[Visit Bloomberg's What Works Cities](https://whatworkscities.bloomberg.org/about-us/)

 
 
 
 

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
### 311

 
Urban AI relaunched 311 as the front door to City Hall for residents with a new website and customer service portal integrated with department systems.

### Open Data Portal

 
The City of Cleveland proactively publishes information on City operations and services to our Open Data Portal—a website where anyone can view or download data.

### 2025 Special Achievement in GIS (SAG) Award

 
We’re excited to share some great news: the City of Cleveland has been awarded the **2025 Special Achievement in GIS (SAG) Award** from Esri, a global leader in geographic information system (GIS) technology. This award recognizes outstanding work by organizations that use GIS to improve the way they operate and serve their communities—and Cleveland is now among the few cities around the world to receive this honor.

[Read the rest of the story here](/city-hall/departments/innovation-technology/uai/news-press)

 
 
 
 

 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
### 311 wins 2025 Best of Tech Award

 
Cleveland 311 was recognized at this year’s Best of Tech Awards for its thoughtful use of technology to make city services more accessible and responsive. Urban AI is proud of its contributions to Cleveland's tech community as we continue to modernize City Hall.
